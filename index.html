Entrepreneurship Cell

IIT Jodhpur

About Us

Entrepreneurship-Cell IIT Jodhpur is a student body that focuses on developing and promoting entrepreneurial eco-system and start-up skills in creative and potential minds.

  • Focuses on various dimensions of the corporate world that revolves around an entrepreneur.
  • Educate people about different aspects of a business
  • Guide and promote entrepreneurial ideas and provide resources to help them establish, nurture and run their own startup.

Structure

Verticals of E-Cell

Funding Advisory and Incubation

Our task is to look over the activities related to the incubation center of IIT Jodhpur. We help the students in funding their startups and in the implementation of their innovative ideas with the help of an incubation center. We connect aspiring startups with the Incubation Center of IIT Jodhpur so that they get proper mentorship. We organize various pitching events in collaboration with the Incubation Center and Investors so that startups who have developed their models could present their ideas. We help students with funding-related activities for their startups by using the collaborations of E-cell with Institute Incubator and Investors.
