Entrepreneurship Cell

IIT Jodhpur

About Us

Entrepreneurship-Cell IIT Jodhpur is a student body that focuses on developing and promoting entrepreneurial eco-system and start-up skills in creative and potential minds.

  • Focuses on various dimensions of the corporate world that revolves around an entrepreneur.
  • Educate people about different aspects of a business
  • Guide and promote entrepreneurial ideas and provide resources to help them establish, nurture and run their own startup.

Structure

Verticals of E-Cell

Funding Advisory and Incubation

Our task is To look over the activities related to the incubation center of IIT Jodhpur.We help the students in funding their startups and in the implementation of their innovative ideas with the help of an incubation center. We connect aspiring startups with the Incubation Center of IIT Jodhpur so that they get proper mentorship. We Organize various pitching events in collaboration with the Incubation Center and Investors so that startups who have developed their models could present their ideas. We Help students with funding-related activities for their startups by using the collaborations of E-cell with Institute Incubator and Investors.

Institute Innovation Council

Our task is to establish a thriving regional innovation sector, enhancing ones cognitive abilities Create a working environment for identifying ideas and concept pre-incubation, Prepare the institution for the Atal Institutional Ranking. To Divide events throughout the year uniformly.so that events fall according to the IIC calendar. Establish connections with peers and national entrepreneurship organisations. Find and reward innovations and share their success stories. Create an institution's innovation portal to highlight innovative projects carried out by the institutions faculty and students.

Media and Publicity

Team MnP takes charge of informing the massÂ about Ecell and its activities. Basically, we do the promotion of E-Cell both inside and beyond the IITJ campus using all available media outlets. This team will be in charge of producing excellent native content, audiovisuals, etc. Publicity is crucial because it raises knowledge of and visibility of our cell and conveys the idea that spending time here is valuable. We strive to increase participation, carry out all social media activities, and provide high-quality information about entrepreneurship such as articles, interviews, games, and announcements about the E-cell activities.

Web Development

We represent online presence of E-Cell IIT Jodhpur on the Internet. Our task is to design, develop and maintain E-Cell's Website. We ensure latest updates of the club are reflected upon the website. After all, the website you're currently viewing is our creation !

Events and Competitions

The Events and Competitions Pillar of E-Cell makes sure that events, workshops, and seminars are successfully organised and conducted to help IIT Jodhpur students develop entrepreneurship-related skills. It also makes sure that IITJ students actively participate in various startup-related events across the nation and the world. These activities are aimed at educating and enlightening interested students.

Corporate Relations, Start Up and Marketing

We serve as the front face E-Cell IIT Jodhpur and are responsible for maintaining healthy relationship with counterparts of E-cell in different colleges, various companies and start-ups. Ensure the availability of mentorship and investment for our college start-ups, sponsorship for events conducted by our E-Cell. Open to collaboration with companies, start-ups and E-cells.
